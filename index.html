P A K A G E S

Mumbai

Mumbai Tourism: A Vibrant Metropolis A city of dreams: Mumbai's bustling energy and diverse culture have made it a symbol of aspiration for millions. A cinematic wonderland: Home to the Indian film industry, Bollywood, Mumbai is a hub of entertainment.

RAJASTHAN

Udaipur Tourism: A Fairytale Come True A city of lakes and palaces: Udaipur's serene lakes, opulent palaces, and lush gardens create a truly enchanting atmosphere. A romantic getaway: The city's picturesque beauty and romantic ambiance make it a popular destination for couples.

UTTRAKHAND

Uttarakhand Tourism: A Himalayan Paradise A land of mountains and rivers: Uttarakhand's majestic Himalayas, pristine rivers, and lush valleys offer breathtaking scenery. A spiritual retreat: Home to sacred Hindu shrines like Kedarnath, Badrinath, and Haridwar.

KERALA

Kerala Tourism: God's Own Country A tropical paradise: Kerala's lush landscapes, backwaters, and pristine beaches offer a serene escape. A cultural melting pot: Experience Kerala's rich cultural heritage through its traditional dance forms, music, and cuisine.

GOA

Goa Tourism: A Tropical Paradise A coastal haven: Goa's pristine beaches, turquoise waters, and palm-fringed shores offer a tropical paradise. A party destination: Known for its vibrant nightlife, beach parties, and music festivals.

JAMMU AND KASHMIR

he beauty and essence of Jammu and Kashmir: A land of contrasts: From the lush valleys of Kashmir to the rugged beauty of Ladakh. A paradise on Earth: With its snow-capped peaks, serene lakes, and vibrant culture. A melting pot of cultures: Home to diverse communities, each with its own unique traditions.

Shiv Shakti Hostel ( UK )

About this hotel Facing forested mountains, this low-key hostel is a minute’s walk from the Ganges River, a 2-minute walk from Lakshman Jhula suspension bridge and 20 km from Neelkanth Mahadev Temple, a hilltop Hindu temple.

Emarald Resort ( kerel)

About this hotel Set off the banks of the Kunthi River, this traditional spa resort in a tropical-like setting lies 8 km from Aligarh Muslim University and 13 km from Thirumandhamkunnu Temple. Bright rooms include free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TVs, desks, and tea and coffeemaking facilities. Suites add separate living areas, while cottages come with balconies. Room service is available 24/7.
